About the Dish

This special dish combines authentic flavors and fresh spices to bring joy to your taste buds. Whether you're a foodie or a casual diner, this dish will leave you craving more!

Recipe & Ingredients

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh vegetables
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • Spices & seasoning
  • Fresh herbs
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  2. Add vegetables and sauté for 5 minutes.
  3. Mix in cooked rice and spices.
  4. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with herbs & serve hot!
